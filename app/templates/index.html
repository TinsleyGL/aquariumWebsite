Live Data Monitoring

Monitor your aquarium water paramters in real time. Use the AquaLogger dashboard to maintian your aquarium.

Live data montering of water parameters such as temperature, pH, filter water flow, and water clarity. The AquaLogger sensor device monitors all of the above and transmits the data to your dashboard, with no intervention required by you.

Historical Data

get access to histroical water parameter data covering temperature, pH, filter water flow, and water clarity

With the AquaLogger graphs, you can monitor your aquarium's water parameter history direct from the internet. Historical data is provided for weekly views, a monthly breakdown, and a yearly breakdown. Each graph provides data for each of the water parameters being monitored, giving you full details.

Social Media

Engage with other users to share in your hobby

With the AquaLogger newsfeed, you can view other people's aquariums from around the world, ask them questions about the hobby, and post direct to the newsfeed yourself.
